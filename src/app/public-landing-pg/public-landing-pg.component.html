The Art of Reduction

It's the peak hour. You're standing on a crowded public bus, only to look out of the window and notice the spacious-looking car next to you is empty, save for one driver.

Thousands of vehicles like this exist on the road every morning, causing traffic buildup and congestions, and increasing the emission of greenhouse gases. Vehicles like these are exactly the type of resource that Tompang seeks to maximise.
